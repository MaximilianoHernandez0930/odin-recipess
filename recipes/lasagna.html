Classic Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna is a traditional Italian comfort food loved around the world. With layers of rich meat sauce, creamy cheese, and tender pasta, it’s a perfect dish for family gatherings, special occasions, or a cozy dinner at home.

This classic lasagna recipe is easy to follow and guaranteed to impress!

Tools and ingredients for this recipe :

Ingredients:

For the sauce:

  • 500g (1lb) ground beef
  • a small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 800g (28 az) canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

For the cheese mixture:

  • 400g (14 oz) ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Other:

  • 9-12 lasagna noodles (pre-cooked or no-boil type)
  • Fresh bails leaves (optional, for garnish)

Tools:

  • Large skilett or frying pan
  • Latge pot (if cooking noodles)
  • Mixing bowls
  • Wooden spoon or spaluta
  • Measuring cups and spoons
  • Knife and cutting board
  • Aluminum foil

Steps:

  1. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat
  2. Add diced onion and cook until soft
  3. Add minced garlic and cook for another 30 seconds
  4. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, salt, pepper, oregano and baisl.
  5. Let it simmer for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.

    6. Prepare the cheese mixture

  6. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, and 1/2 cup of Parmesan cheese. Mix well.

    7. Preheat the oven

  7. Preheat your oven to 190°C(375°F)

    8. Assemble the lasagna

  8. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce at the bottom of the baking dish
  9. Place a layer of noodles over the sauce
  10. Spread half of the ricotta mixture over the noodles
  11. Sprinkle 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese
  12. Repeat the layers: sauce, noodles, ricotta, mozzarella
  13. Finish with a layer of noodles, remaining meat sauce, and the rest of the mozzarella and Parmesan cheese

    14. Bake

  14. Cover the lasagna with aluminium foil (spray the underside with cooking spray to avoid sticking)
  15. Bake for 25 minutes
  16. Remove the foil and bake for another 20 minutes until the cheese is golden and bubbly

    17. Rest and serve

  17. Let the lasagna rest for at least 10 minutes before cutting
  18. Garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired
  19. Serve and enjoy
