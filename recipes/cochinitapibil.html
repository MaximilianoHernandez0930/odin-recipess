Mexican Cochinita Pibil

Cochinita pibil is a slow-roasted pork dish from Yucatán, Mexico, marinated in achiote and citrus, then wrapped in banana leaves and traditionally cooked underground,is ideal to share with family

Ingredients and Tools:

Ingredients:

    For the pork:

  • 1.5-2kg(3-4lb) pork shoulder or butt, cut into large chunks
  • 100g (3.5 oz) achiote paste
  • 1 cup orange juice (preferably sour orange or a mix of orange and lime juice)
  • 1/4 cup white vinegar
  • 5 garlic cloves
  • 1 tablespoon oregano (preferably Mexican)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • Banana leaves (or aluminum foil if unaviable)

    • For pickled red onions (optional):

  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup white vinegar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 pinch of oregano or allspice (optional)

    • To serve:

  • Corn tortillas
  • Habanero salsa(optional)
  • Black beans (optional)

Tools:

  • Blender or food processor
  • Large mixing bowl
  • Baking dish or Dutch oven
  • Aluminum foil or banana leaves
  • Knife and cutting board
  • Oven
  • Tongs or seving spoon

Steps:

    Make the marinade:

  1. In a blender, mix achiote paste, orange juice, vinegar, garlic, oregano, cumin, and salt. Blend until smooth.

    2. Marinate the pork:

  2. Place pork chunks in a large bowl or baking dish.
  3. Pour the marinade over the meat and mix to coat evenly.
  4. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, preferably overnight

    5. Wrap and cook:

  5. preheat the oven to 160°C (320°F).
  6. Line a baking dish with banana leaves (or foil), place the pork and marinade inside, and cover with more leaves or foil.
  7. Cover tightly and bake for 3-4 hours until the pork is tender and shreds easily.

    8. Prepare pickled onions(optional):

  8. Combine sliced red onions, vinegar, water, salt, and optional spices in a bowl.
  9. Let sit for at least 30 minutes.

    10. Shred and serve:

  10. Shred the pork with two forks and mix with its juices
  11. Serve on warm corn tortillas with pickled onions, salsa and beans if desired
